# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want LabelHub to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community:
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Welcoming newcomers and helping them get started.
- Gracefully accepting feedback and focusing on what is best for the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainer at
**ge.bondar.robot@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and handled
confidentially. The maintainer may remove comments, commits, issues, or
contributors that violate this Code of Conduct.
